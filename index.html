Experience

SynchroGrid

Currently, at SynchroGrid, I am engaged in designing protective relay power systems for different clients, utilizing Siemens CAPE/Gridscale X. My role involves setting relay parameters for power line protection, simulating power system designs to ensure protection device efficacy, and analyzing SEL relay protection settings for optimal safety and reliability.

Valero

At Valero, I developed a re-feeding plan to shift loads from overhead lines, significantly reducing power outages and saving $162,500 daily. My work also included collaborating with CenterPoint on substation and electrical equipment upgrades for tank farm backup power, designing a 4160 V power plan for CAT cooler motors, and managing the installation of a new power feeder line to the crude tank farm, bolstering the refinery's power infrastructure.

SAMSUNG

For my senior design project with Samsung, I led a team to automate generator testing at Austin's fabrication labs, achieving a 65% cost reduction. I developed an Android application with advanced testing features and data analytics, which streamlined generator testing. This project earned first place in the Texas A&M Board of Directors’ competition.

SIEMENS

During my internship at Siemens in Atlanta, I worked on optimizing the Dust Test Chamber by redesigning its air regulation system, addressing mechanical issues, and achieving UL certification for appliance control testing. I also contributed to developing DC testing capabilities to support hybrid AC/DC grid demands, conducting capacitor discharge and short-circuit tests, and exploring cost-effective solutions for external testing. Additionally, I assisted in designing a short-circuit simulation model in MATLAB Simulink, integrating magnetic, rotor, and arc voltage data to provide customers with a cost-effective and efficient analysis tool.
